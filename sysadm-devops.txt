Rocky Linux ( June 2021) is a new community-maintained distro which is intended to replace CentOS, leaded by the original developer of CentOS. 
Rocky Linux 8 is intended to be supported upto 2029. Basically CentOS with new name but not fully-fledged.

https://blog.cloudlinux.com/announcing-open-sourced-community-driven-rhel-fork-by-cloudlinux
AlmaLinux (March 2021) is also started for same intention, maintained by CloudLinux, a company which also has a commercial offering CloudLinux OS. 
Their FAQ says "CloudLinux is committed to supporting AlmaLinux until 2029".
